### On which browsers the extension can be installed?

**Compatible with:**
- Chrome & Chrome Canary
- Chromium
- Edge (from 2020)
- Opera
- Vivaldi
- Brave
- Yandex
- [...and many browsers based on chromium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chromium_(web_browser)#Browsers_based_on_Chromium)

**Incompatible with:**
- Firefox
- Safari
